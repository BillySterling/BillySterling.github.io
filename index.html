About Me

I am a Web Development professional with extensive experience in the development, maintenance, and management of business applications. My I.T. career experience has predominantly been in online banking applications and airline flight scheduling applications. Web development has always fascinated me and the time was right to make the transition into this field. I completed the Georgia Tech full stack coding bootcamp where I acquired skills in HTML, CSS, javascript/JQuery, and SQL.

I believe that my technical skills combined with my ability to effectively communicate and collaborate with both colleagues and clients towards the fulfillment of goals makes me a valuable asset to any team.

My hobbies include woodworking and DIY home projects, music, and all things motorcycle. Hands on and not afraid to get said hands dirty, always trying to do the best job possible.

Thanks for taking the time to visit my page, and please check out my projects on the portfolio page.
