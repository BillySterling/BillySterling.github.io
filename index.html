About Me

I am a Web Development professional with extensive experience in the development, maintenance, and management of business applications. My I.T. career experience has predominantly been in online banking and airline flight scheduling applications. Web development has always fascinated me and the time was right to make the transition into this field. I completed the Georgia Tech full stack coding bootcamp where I acquired skills in frontend and backend web development

I believe that my technical skills combined with my ability to effectively communicate and collaborate with both colleagues and clients towards the fulfillment of goals makes me a valuable asset to any team.

My hobbies include woodworking and DIY home projects, music, and all things motorcycle. Hands on and not afraid to get said hands dirty, always trying to do the best job possible.
